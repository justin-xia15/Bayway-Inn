Rooms

Our spacious rooms are designed for comfort and come equipped with elegant furniture, including a king size bed. Enjoy a stunning view of the world famous Waikiki Beach from your private balcony.


Dining

Our elegantly designed restaurant, the Waikiki Grill, is conveniently located on the main floor of the hotel. We provide a diverse menu with traditional Hawaiian foods and an extensive wine list.


Amenities

A complimentary continental breakfast is provided for all guests. We also provide many other amenities such as high speed internet.

High speed internet

At Bayway Inn, we understand how frustrating slow internet can be. All of our rooms come equipped with a fast internet connection.

Room service

Don't hesitate to give the front desk a call if you require assistance. Our staff would love to make your stay at Bayway Inn as enjoyable as possible.

Laundry and Dry Cleaning

24-hour laundry and dry cleaning services are provided. We also offer a laundry pick-up service. Your laundry will be cleaned and delivered straight to your hotel room.
